AI Powered Yoga App

Did you know?

Yoga is about 5000 years old and one of the ancient and proven forms of exercise for gaining fitness.

    Sensai is:
  • An AI powered web application that addresses significant pain point in yoga by providing accurate and personalized feedback on alignment and posture
  • Non-intrusive solution that depends on your willingness to share data
  • Leverages the latest advancements in ML and CV to detect subtlest movements and provide customized feedback to users.
